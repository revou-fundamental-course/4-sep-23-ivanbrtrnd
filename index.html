Hello everyone! Welcome to KaMa,
a simple web app to calculate the perimeter and area of a square.

Square Perimeter

The formula for the perimeter of a square is:

P = 4 × l

Where:
P = Perimeter
l = Length of sides

Square Area

The formula for the Area of a square is:

A = l × l

Where:
A = Area
l = Length of sides
